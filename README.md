-The webpage consists of three separate pages;

    -Portfolio

-The pages are divided into three sections;

    -main section
    -footer
-The point of this project is to use it as a portfolio to showcase all the work thats being completed in the class. By taking a look at this website, other people are able to get an idea of who I am and to see the talent that I possess in web development.
